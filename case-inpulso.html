Case study

Inpulso 43

A complete digital ecosystem for a human development center — from cinematic marketing to a virtual campus, workshop store, and AI-powered reception.

Live in production 2024 — 2026 Wellness · Education · Healthcare
Visit inpulso43.com ↗ Workshops page ↗

Before

Inpulso 43 is a human development center in Zapopan, Mexico — offering psychology, nutrition, family medicine, and workshops. They had fragmented digital presence: basic pages, manual WhatsApp handling, no online payments, and no way to deliver courses or certificates digitally.

What they needed

  • A professional website that builds trust and converts visitors into clients
  • Online workshop registration and payment processing
  • A virtual campus for students with verifiable certificates
  • Automated customer support via WhatsApp and web chat
  • SEO to be found on Google for local wellness searches
  • An admin panel to manage content without a developer

What I built

Marketing site

40+ PHP pages with cinematic GSAP animations, Lenis smooth scroll, Swiper carousels, and glass-morphism UI.

Workshop store

Talleres page with premium gallery, Stripe payments, waitlist management, and WhatsApp integration.

Virtual campus (LMS)

Course delivery, progress tracking, and verifiable certificates with public validation URLs.

Tu Espacio

Social area with user profiles, feed, blog, and community features backed by SQLite.

Custom CMS

Admin panel to manage site content, users, courses, and digital products without touching code.

Podcast section

Dynamic podcast page with video cards, caching, and sync scripts for content updates.

Alessia AI assistant

Floating web chat widget connected to the same API as WhatsApp — unified customer experience.

Deployment pipeline

SUBIR-AL-HOSTING folder with flat production copy and FTP scripts for Hostinger deployment.

AI integration

Every "contact us" button on the site routes to Alessia — an AI assistant I built separately that handles WhatsApp and web chat. It books appointments in Google Calendar, manages workshop waitlists, sends reminders, and escalates to humans when needed.

SEO & performance

  • Dynamic meta tags and Open Graph for every page
  • Schema markup and structured data for services
  • Clean URLs via Apache .htaccess rewrite rules
  • Optimized images, preconnect hints, and lazy loading
  • Mobile-first responsive design across all modules
