My story

I'm a software development engineer focused on building real products for real businesses — not demos that never ship.

My flagship project is Inpulso 43, a complete digital platform for a wellness center in Mexico: marketing site, virtual campus, workshop store, custom CMS, and integrated AI assistant. I also built Alessia, an open-source WhatsApp and web chat assistant with 25+ business tools running on DigitalOcean.

Before coding, I think about the business outcome: Will this get more clients? Save reception hours? Make the brand look trustworthy? That mindset shapes every project I take.
